About Us

Climate Action Now: a non profit organization that acts for the environment

Our Mission

Our mission is to inform people about the impact fast fashion and clothing industry have on the environment and also to call to action for buying more thrifted clothes

By being a part of the call to action, which means buying more second hand clothes and also boycotting clothing companies that contribute on the environment pollution there is going to be less consumption of resources (water and energy for production).

Moreover, by utilizing resources and energy that were already used, the demand for new things is not directly supported and the life of the objects gets extended. Also the amount of items that end up in the trash are getting less and the chemical pollution is being reduced.
